A practical framework for integrating generative AI into enterprise support and troubleshooting

Here's a mental model that's served me well: think of AI agents as brilliant interns who've read every textbook but have never touched a production system.

They have encyclopedic knowledge. They can synthesize information across domains. They're eager to help. But they have zero practical experience, no institutional context, and no intuition for what actually works in your environment.

The Smart Intern Problem

You wouldn't hand an intern the keys to production and walk away. You'd give them structured tasks, review their work, and use your expertise to catch the things they couldn't possibly know.

The goal isn't to replace technical expertise with AI. It's to amplify your expertise by offloading the cognitive load of synthesis.
